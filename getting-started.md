# Getting Started with Revoya

Welcome to Revoya! This guide will help you get up and running with automated compliance monitoring.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, you'll need:

- A Revoya account (sign up at [app.revoya.ai](https://app.revoya.ai))
- Admin access to your organization's repositories or cloud infrastructure
- Basic familiarity with your compliance framework (SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, etc.)

## Quick Start

### 1. Create Your Account

Visit [app.revoya.ai](https://app.revoya.ai) and sign up with your work email.

### 2. Connect Your Integrations

Revoya supports multiple integration types:

- **GitHub** - Scan your code repositories
- **AWS** - Monitor your cloud infrastructure
- **Kubernetes** - Check your container deployments

Navigate to **Settings â†’ Integrations** and connect your services.

### 3. Select Your Frameworks

Choose which compliance frameworks apply to your organization:

- SOC 2 Type II
- ISO 27001
- HIPAA
- PCI DSS
- Custom frameworks

### 4. Run Your First Scan

Once connected, Revoya will automatically begin scanning your infrastructure. You'll see results in your dashboard within minutes.
